Our Services

Comprehensive cleaning and maintenance solutions for every commercial need.

Full-Service Facility Care

Whether your building needs daily janitorial attention, post-construction detail work, or specialized deep cleaning, SSJ has the expertise and equipment to deliver outstanding results.

Janitorial Services

Daily, weekly, and custom-scheduled cleaning programs designed around your building's specific traffic patterns and operational hours.

Construction Cleanup

Thorough post-construction detail cleaning that transforms new and renovated spaces into move-in-ready environments.

Carpet Cleaning

Professional hot-water extraction and encapsulation methods that restore carpet appearance and extend fiber life across high-traffic commercial floors.

Power Washing

High-pressure exterior cleaning for building facades, sidewalks, parking structures, and loading docks that restores curb appeal and removes stubborn buildup.

Parking Lot Striping

Precise line striping, stenciling, and ADA-compliant markings that enhance safety, organization, and regulatory compliance for your parking facilities.

Vapor Cleaning

Chemical-free steam sanitation technology that penetrates surfaces at high temperatures, eliminating bacteria and allergens without harsh residues.

Event Cleaning

Pre-event preparation and post-event restoration for conferences, galas, and corporate gatherings — ensuring your venue is impeccable from setup to breakdown.

Specialty Services

From window washing and hard floor refinishing to light fixture cleaning and ceiling tile replacement — we handle the tasks others overlook.

Disinfecting Services

Hospital-grade electrostatic and fogging disinfection protocols that provide thorough pathogen elimination for high-risk and high-traffic environments.

Tailored to Your Building

No two facilities are alike, and neither are our cleaning programs. Our operations team works directly with you to develop a scope of work that matches your building's layout, occupancy, and operational schedule.

We continuously refine our approach based on inspection feedback, seasonal demands, and any changes to your space — because great facility care isn't static, it evolves with your business.

Need a Custom Cleaning Program?

Tell us about your facility and we'll design a service plan that fits your building, budget, and quality standards.
