About SSJ Commercial Services

Building trust through exceptional facility care since day one.

Who We Are

SSJ Commercial Services was founded on a simple principle: every commercial space deserves outstanding care. Based in Nevada, we have grown into a trusted partner for property managers, building owners, and facility directors who refuse to settle for anything less than excellence.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in commercial janitorial services, construction cleanup, and specialized facility maintenance. We invest in people, training, and technology so that every property we serve reflects the professional image our clients demand.

Our Mission

To deliver consistent, reliable, and environmentally responsible facility maintenance that enhances the value and appearance of every property we serve.

We measure success not by the number of buildings on our roster, but by the trust and satisfaction of the people inside them. Our goal is to become an invisible extension of your operations — working quietly in the background so your team can focus on what matters most.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every property we maintain.

Integrity

We do the right thing, even when no one is watching. Honesty and transparency are the foundation of every client relationship.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards. Every floor, surface, and corner receives the same meticulous attention to detail.

Sustainability

Green cleaning isn't optional — it's core to how we work. We protect occupant health and the environment with every product and process we choose.

People First

Our employees are our greatest asset. We invest in ongoing training, safety programs, and career growth to build a team you can trust.

Accountability

We own our results. Regular inspections, client feedback loops, and performance metrics ensure we keep our promises.

Reliability

Consistency is everything in facility maintenance. We show up on time, every time, with the right people and the right equipment.

Built on Trust & Quality

Every client relationship we build starts with trust and grows through consistent, measurable performance. We don't ask you to take our word for it — we prove it through transparent reporting, regular quality audits, and open communication.

Our management team conducts routine site inspections, reviews cleaning logs, and meets with clients regularly to ensure expectations are not just met but exceeded. This commitment to quality is why our clients stay with us year after year.
